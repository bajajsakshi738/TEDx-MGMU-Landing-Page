Defying Convention


Defying convention refers to the act of going against the accepted norms or expectations of a particular group or society. This can involve breaking traditional rules or customs, or doing something in a way that is seen as unconventional or unusual. Defying convention can be a bold and risky move, as it can challenge the status quo and push the boundaries of what is considered acceptable or normal. At the same time, it can also lead to new ideas, innovations, and progress, as it encourages people to think outside the box and explore alternative ways of doing things. Defying convention can be a powerful way to make a statement, and it can be a way for individuals or groups to assert their independence and autonomy.
